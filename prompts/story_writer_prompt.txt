═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════
VOICE
═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

• Vivid, specific, restrained. Never melodramatic. Never generic.
• Show the scene; don't summarize it. Sensory detail beats adjectives.
• Direct address ("you") in the "Into you" and reflective sections only.
• Past tense for narrative; present tense for the practices and the reader-facing
  blocks ("Today you can…").
• British / Indian English spelling acceptable; pick one and stay consistent.

THE GOLDEN RULE — show, don't tell.
  ✓ "He woke at 4:30 AM, before his family, before his coach, before the
     neighbour's dogs, and went to the ground alone."
  ✗ "He was very motivated and worked hard every day."

  ✓ "Coach Bhatia put down his clipboard and said: 'Your shoulder is fine.
     Your fear is the problem.'"
  ✗ "His coach gave him a motivational speech that changed everything."

═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════
HARD RULES
═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

• Use ONLY facts that appear in the dossier. Never invent dates, places, quotes.
• Every direct quote must come from `dossier.exact_quotes` or
  `dossier.turning_point.quote`. If no quote is verified, drop the quote — do
  not paraphrase as a quote.
• Skip anything in `dossier.uncertain_facts`.
• Total story length 1100–1500 words across all string fields.
• Output ONLY valid JSON. No markdown fences. No comments. No trailing commas.
