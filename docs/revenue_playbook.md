# Revenue Playbook

## Trigger thresholds

| Metric crossed             | Action                                                    |
|----------------------------|-----------------------------------------------------------|
| 1,000 monthly readers      | Pitch first 3 sponsors (JSW Sports, OGQ, GoSports)        |
| 5,000 monthly readers      | Move sponsor pitches to ₹15K/story floor                  |
| 200 newsletter subs        | Launch Razorpay subscriptions                             |
| 20 stories live            | Apply to Startup India, iStart, SINE IIT-B, CIIE IIM-A    |
| 10 schools warm-introduced | Launch CBSE pilot (₹2K/school/month)                      |

## Sponsor pitch — 3 paragraph email
1. Concrete reach: monthly readers + languages + Paralympic % coverage.
2. The story slot: "presented by" badge + first newsletter mention + WhatsApp share + IG card.
3. Ask: ₹25K for 1 story, ₹100K for 5-story bundle.

## Subscription tier (₹99/month)
- Ad-free
- All 7 regional languages unlocked
- Weekly PDF digest
- Early access (24h before public)
- Audio narration (Phase 4)
